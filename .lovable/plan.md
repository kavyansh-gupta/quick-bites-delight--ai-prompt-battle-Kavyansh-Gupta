

# Quick Bites - Food Website UI

## Overview
A clean, customer-friendly food website called **Quick Bites** with a warm cream and tomato red color scheme, featuring appetizing food imagery and easy navigation.

## Color Scheme
- **Background:** Soft Cream (#FFF8F0)
- **Primary:** Tomato Red (#E63946)
- **Secondary:** Warm Orange (#F4A261)
- **Text:** Dark Charcoal (#2B2B2B)

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar (sticky)
- Quick Bites logo/brand name on the left
- Navigation links: **Home**, **Menu**, **Offers**, **Contact**
- **Login** button (highlighted in primary red)

### 2. Homepage — Hero Section
- Large hero banner with an appetizing food image
- Catchy tagline (e.g., "Delicious Food, Delivered Fast")
- Call-to-action **"Browse Categories"** button

### 3. Homepage — Browse Categories
- Grid of food category cards (e.g., Burgers, Pizza, Salads, Desserts, Drinks, Pasta)
- Each card with a food image, category name, and hover effect

### 4. Homepage — Our Menu Section
- Section heading: **"Our Menu"**
- Grid of menu item cards showing:
  - Food image
  - Item name
  - Short description
  - Price
  - "Order Now" button

### 5. Homepage — Offers/Deals Banner
- Highlighted section showcasing current deals with vibrant imagery

### 6. Homepage — Footer / Social Media Bar
- Social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, YouTube)
- "Connect with us" text
- Copyright and Quick Bites branding

### 7. Menu Page
- Full menu listing organized by categories with food images and prices

### 8. Offers Page
- Current deals and promotional offers displayed as cards

### 9. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, message)
- Restaurant address, phone, and email info

### 10. Login Page
- Simple login form with email and password fields

## Design Approach
- Fully responsive (mobile-friendly)
- Placeholder food images throughout using Unsplash
- Smooth hover animations on cards and buttons
- Warm, inviting aesthetic matching the color palette
